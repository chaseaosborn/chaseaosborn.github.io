Chase's Movie Mania

Alien (1979)

Genre: Science Fiction/Horror

Director: Ridley Scott

Runtime: 116 Minutes

Rating:


Synopsis

The crew of a commercial spacecraft called the "Nostromo" encounters a deadly lifeform after investigating a mysterious transmission of unknown origin. This deadly lifeform then terrorizes the crew of the spacecraft as it picks them apart one by one until there is only one sole survivor remaining. That sole survivor, Ellen Ripley (Sigourney Weaver), must fend off and survive the creature all alone and try to get it off the ship.

Howard the Duck (1986)

Genre: Science Fiction/Comedy

Director: Willard Huyck

Runtime: 111 minutes

Rating:


Synopsis

Based on the character from Marvel Comics, a sarcastic humanoid duck is pulled from his homeworld to Earth, more specifically Cleveland, Ohio, where he must stop an alien invasion with the help of a nerdy scientist (Tim Robbins) and a struggling female rock singer (Lea Thompson) while he himself wants to find a way to get back to his homeworld while struggling to fit in on Earth as well.

The Goonies (1985)

Genre: Adventure/Comedy

Director: Richard Donner

Runtime: 114 Minutes

Rating:


Synopsis

Facing foreclosure on their homes, a group of kids who call themselves "the Goonies" discover a treasure map from a legendary pirate called "One Eyed Willy". Figuring that the treasure could help their neighborhood from the foreclosure, they head off to follow the map, but they must overcome various obstacles along the way that include a crime family that also wants in on the treasure for themselves.
